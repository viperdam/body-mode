Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Body Mode. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your health and fitness information when you use our mobile application.

Body Mode is an AI-powered health and fitness coach that helps you track meals, workouts, sleep, and hydration to achieve your wellness goals.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health & Fitness Data

We collect the following types of data to provide personalized recommendations:

  • Physical Metrics: Age, gender, height, weight, body measurements
  • Activity Data: Daily step count, workout sessions, exercise types, activity levels detected by your device
  • Nutrition Information: Food photos, meal logs, calorie intake, macronutrient data
  • Sleep Data: Sleep duration, sleep quality metrics, bedtime and wake time
  • Hydration: Water intake logs and hydration tracking
  • Goals & Preferences: Fitness goals, dietary preferences, target weights

2.2 Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Mobile network information
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

2.3 Photos & Camera Access

When you use our AI food recognition feature, we process photos you take of your meals. These photos are sent to our AI service for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers after processing.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • AI-Powered Recommendations: Generate personalized meal plans, workout routines, and health insights
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your fitness journey and show you progress over time
  • Smart Reminders: Send hydration reminders and activity notifications
  • Feature Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and user experience
  • Food Recognition: Process meal photos through AI to identify foods and estimate nutritional content
  • Chat Assistance: Provide AI-powered health and fitness guidance through our chat feature

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Personal profile information
  • Daily activity logs
  • Meal and workout history
  • Sleep records

4.2 Cloud Processing

When you use AI features (meal analysis, plan generation, chat), your data is temporarily sent to our secure cloud functions powered by Google's Gemini AI. This data is:

  • Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Used only for generating your personalized results
  • Not shared with third parties for advertising purposes

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure serverless architecture
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No permanent storage of sensitive health photos

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

5.1 AI Processing Service

We use Google's Gemini AI API to power our intelligent features. When you use AI features, your data is processed according to Google's privacy policies and is not used to train AI models or shared with other users.

5.2 No Data Selling

We never sell your personal health data to third parties. Your fitness journey is private and belongs to you.

5.3 Analytics

We may use anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes to understand how users interact with Body Mode and improve our services. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Modification: Edit or update your profile and health information
  • Deletion: Delete specific entries or clear all your data from the app
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (feature coming soon)
  • Opt-Out: Disable specific features like activity tracking or notifications

7. Permissions & Device Access

Body Mode requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos of meals for AI food recognition (optional)
  • Activity Recognition: To automatically detect sleep and physical activities (optional)
  • Notifications: To send hydration reminders and activity alerts (optional)
  • Storage: To save your data locally on your device

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings. Some features may not function without the relevant permissions.

8. Children's Privacy

Body Mode is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Medical Disclaimer

Important: Body Mode provides health and fitness tracking tools and AI-generated recommendations, but it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider before making significant changes to your diet or exercise routine.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, providing notice through the app.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@bodymode.app
  • Website: bodymode.netlify.app

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
